Intro

What I am all about.

Hi! I’m Jessica, Front‑end Web Developer & Freelancer. Born and raised in The Netherlands and moved to this tropical paradise called Miami in 2016. Besides my love for software engineering, I am a big Travel & Photography enthousiast. With 23 visited countries on my list, I plan to see many many more in the near future.

My passion for creating websites started while making a Wordpress website for one of my own projects. After coming from a Hospitality & Leisure Management background, I was so eager to learn more about programming, that I decided to quit my job and dive into it fulltime by starting a Full Stack Web Development bootcamp at Ironhack (Miami) in May 2017.

I have been working as a Front‑end Developer since and as a Freelancer who is ready to take on your next project.
